Term 2 Reflection

Term 2 of Business Information Technology was a lot more hands-on and technical than Term 1. The classes became less general, and started to focus on higher-level understandings of the concepts in each class. The classes also noticeably built off each other at times. For example, we were learning Events in C# around the same time we were learning EventListeners in JavaScript. This strengthened our understanding of both concepts in both classes. I am very much looking forward to Term 3 in the coming Fall 2022. :)
